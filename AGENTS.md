# AGENTS.md

## Repository purpose
This repository is an Obsidian vault for Korean writing, research notes, and article drafts.

## Important folders
- `00_Inbox/`: temporary ideas and uncategorized notes
- `01_Daily/`: daily notes
- `02_Study/`: evergreen notes and concepts
- `03_Blog/`: article ideas, drafts, revisions, published writing
- `04_Projects/`: long-form writing projects
- `05_CodingTest/`: CodingTest summery
- `06_Resources/`: source notes, quotes, media, PDFs
- `90_Templates/`: Obsidian templates
- `99_System/`: indexes, dashboards, review logs

## Writing rules
- Write in Korean unless the target file says otherwise.
- Preserve YAML frontmatter.
- Do not edit `.obsidian/` unless explicitly asked.
- Do not invent citations, sources, quotes, statistics, or book/page references.
- If a draft uses source notes, cite them as Obsidian links like `[[Source Title]]`.
- Prefer clear structure, short paragraphs, and concrete examples.
- Keep the author's voice; improve clarity without making the prose generic.

## Workflow
- For major rewrites, propose an outline first.
- For edits, summarize changed files and the reason for each change.
- Before finishing, check for:
  - unsupported claims
  - repeated ideas
  - weak title
  - unclear opening
  - abrupt ending
